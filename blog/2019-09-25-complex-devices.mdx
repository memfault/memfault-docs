## ChibiOS Support

Memfault now has support for accepting and parsing coredumps from a device
running ChibiOS. It will recover threads, thread state, and global and static
variables.

<!-- truncate -->

## Support for Complex Devices

Memfault has gained new functionality related to Devices with complex
architectures, such as having multiple MCU's or multiple software packages.

Check out our our Help page linked below to learn more.

## Device Event Filtering

It is now easier to filter Events by Device. Just click the magnifying glass
next to the Device Serial and filter away!
